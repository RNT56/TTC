# Community conduct

Participation in ForgedTTC spaces requires professional, technically honest, and
safe collaboration.

Be respectful; critique work rather than people; disclose uncertainty and conflicts
of interest; protect private and security-sensitive information; respect licenses and
provenance; and do not pressure anyone to weaken validation, privacy, export, dual-use,
or hardware-safety gates.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, deliberate
misrepresentation of evidence, unsafe hardware encouragement, and prohibited
weapons/targeting contributions are not accepted.

Report ordinary conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub.
Report security matters through [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md). Maintainers may edit,
hide, reject, or remove contributions and restrict participation when necessary to
protect people, the project, or its evidence/safety boundaries. Enforcement decisions
should record the relevant behavior, impact, and proportionate response without
unnecessary publication of personal information.
